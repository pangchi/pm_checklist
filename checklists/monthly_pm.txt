# Monthly Preventive Maintenance Checklist
# Frequency: Monthly | Est. Duration: 1–2 hours

SECTION: Safety & Permit Verification

NOTE: Obtain permit-to-work before starting. Verify all isolations are in place.
STEP: Permit-to-work obtained and signed
STEP: Lock Out / Tag Out (LOTO) applied
STEP: Safety data sheets (SDS) reviewed for chemicals in use
STEP_VALUE: Record ambient temperature (°C)
STEP_VALUE: Record ambient humidity (%RH)

SECTION: Visual Inspection

STEP: Inspect equipment exterior for damage, leaks, or corrosion
STEP: Check all labels, warning signs, and safety stickers are legible
STEP: Verify all guards and covers are in good condition
PHOTO: Photograph overall equipment condition

SECTION: Mechanical Checks

STEP: Inspect all mechanical fasteners — tighten any loose bolts/screws
STEP: Check belts, chains, and drives for wear or misalignment
STEP_VALUE: Record belt tension measurement (N or Hz)
STEP: Inspect all hoses and tubing for cracks or leaks
STEP: Clean all air vents and filters

SECTION: Electrical Checks

STEP: Inspect power cables and connectors for damage
STEP: Verify all indicator lights and alarms are functional
STEP_VALUE: Measure and record supply voltage (VAC)
STEP: Test emergency stop (E-Stop) — verify correct operation

SECTION: Lubrication & Consumables

STEP_VALUE: Record lubricant type used
STEP_VALUE: Record lubricant quantity applied (mL)
STEP: Lubricate all designated points per maintenance schedule
PHOTO: Photograph lubrication points after servicing

SECTION: Functional Test

STEP: Power on equipment and observe startup — no abnormal sounds or alarms
STEP_VALUE: Record startup time (seconds)
STEP: Run equipment through one complete operating cycle
STEP_VALUE: Record operating current during run (A)

SECTION: Close-Out

STEP: Work area cleaned, all tools accounted for
STEP: LOTO removed and equipment returned to service
STEP_VALUE: Record total maintenance duration (minutes)
PHOTO: Photograph final equipment condition
STEP: Work order updated with findings and corrective actions
