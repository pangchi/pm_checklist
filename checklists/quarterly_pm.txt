# Quarterly Preventive Maintenance Checklist
# Frequency: Every 3 months | Est. Duration: 3–5 hours

SECTION: Safety & Permit Verification

NOTE: Quarterly PM requires additional isolation review. Confirm with shift supervisor before proceeding.
STEP: Permit-to-work obtained and signed by supervisor
STEP: Lock Out / Tag Out (LOTO) applied and verified by second person
STEP: Safety data sheets (SDS) reviewed for all chemicals/lubricants
STEP_VALUE: Record ambient temperature (°C)
STEP_VALUE: Record ambient humidity (%RH)
PHOTO: Photograph LOTO tags in place

SECTION: Visual & Structural Inspection

STEP: Inspect all structural welds and frames for cracks
STEP: Check all seals and gaskets — replace if worn or hardened
STEP: Inspect all guards, interlocks, and safety devices
STEP: Verify equipment leveling — check and adjust if needed
STEP_VALUE: Record equipment level reading (mm/m)
PHOTO: Photograph any damaged or worn components found

SECTION: Mechanical Overhaul

STEP: Remove and inspect all filters — replace if required
STEP_VALUE: Record filter condition (Good / Marginal / Replaced)
STEP: Inspect and re-torque all critical fasteners per torque spec
STEP_VALUE: Record torque spec used (N·m)
STEP: Inspect bearings for noise, play, or heat — replace if necessary
STEP_VALUE: Record bearing condition (Good / Replaced)
STEP: Inspect all drive belts and couplings — replace as needed
PHOTO: Photograph mechanical components after inspection

SECTION: Electrical & Instrumentation

STEP: Inspect all terminal blocks and connectors — tighten as needed
STEP: Test all limit switches and proximity sensors
STEP: Calibrate or verify calibration of all process instruments
STEP_VALUE: Record instrument calibration due date
STEP_VALUE: Measure supply voltage (VAC)
STEP_VALUE: Measure insulation resistance (MΩ)
STEP: Test all safety interlocks and alarms

SECTION: Fluid Systems

STEP: Inspect hydraulic/pneumatic lines for leaks or abrasion
STEP_VALUE: Record system pressure at operating point (bar / PSI)
STEP: Drain and replace fluids per schedule
STEP_VALUE: Record fluid type and volume replaced (L)
STEP: Inspect all valves for correct operation and no internal leakage
PHOTO: Photograph fluid system after service

SECTION: Lubrication Full Service

NOTE: Use only approved lubricants specified in the equipment manual.
STEP_VALUE: Record lubricant specification (type and grade)
STEP: Clean all old grease/oil from lubrication points before re-greasing
STEP: Lubricate all points per the quarterly schedule
STEP_VALUE: Record total lubricant quantity used (mL)

SECTION: Performance & Functional Test

STEP: Power on equipment — verify startup sequence is normal
STEP_VALUE: Record startup time (seconds)
STEP: Run full operating cycle — record any abnormalities
STEP_VALUE: Record peak operating temperature (°C)
STEP_VALUE: Record operating current at full load (A)
STEP_VALUE: Record cycle time (seconds)
STEP: Verify all alarms, interlocks, and safety devices activate correctly
STEP: Confirm communication with control system / MES is normal
PHOTO: Photograph equipment in operating condition

SECTION: Close-Out & Documentation

STEP: All replaced parts and consumables logged in maintenance record
STEP: Area cleaned and all tools and spare parts accounted for
STEP: LOTO removed and equipment signed back into service
STEP_VALUE: Record total maintenance duration (minutes)
PHOTO: Photograph final equipment condition
STEP: Maintenance report submitted to supervisor for review
